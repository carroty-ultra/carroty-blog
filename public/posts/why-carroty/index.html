Introducing Carroty

December 30, 2025 · 3 min

Why Carroty?#

I wanted an app that could help me plan healthy meals based on my macros. Simple enough, right? If I want to eat 125g of protein each day, I need to know the nutrition facts for each recipe in my meal plan.

Turns out, no app does this well.

The Problem With Existing Apps#

I downloaded most of the meal planning apps on the market. Each one has something going for it, but they all fall short in the same way: they’re either good at meal planning or good at nutrition tracking, never both.

Mealime#

Mealime is genuinely excellent for meal planning. The interface is clean, the recipes are solid, and planning your week is straightforward. But when it comes to nutrition? The details simply aren’t there. You can’t see macros for recipes or meal plans, which makes it useless if you’re trying to hit specific targets.

Recime#

Recime impressed me with its ability to import recipes from any URL—even Instagram posts. That’s a killer feature. But nutrition facts are locked behind a paywall, which isn’t acceptable for something so fundamental. Its meal planning capabilities also lag behind Mealime.

Mealia#

Mealia genuinely surprised me. The supermarket integration is slick—you can add recipe ingredients directly to your basket. But once again, nutrition facts are missing. You’re flying blind on macros.

Cronometer#

Then there’s the other category: nutrition-first apps like Cronometer. It pulls data from proper sources like NCCDB and USDA FoodData Central. The nutrition tracking is thorough. But meal planning? Essentially non-existent. It’s built for logging what you’ve eaten, not planning what you’re going to eat.

What Was Missing#

Every app forced me to choose:

  • Meal planning apps that ignore nutrition
  • Nutrition apps that ignore meal planning

I needed something that balanced both. An app where I could plan my meals for the week and immediately see whether I’m hitting my protein target. Where nutrition facts aren’t an afterthought or a premium feature—they’re the foundation.

That app didn’t exist. So I built it.

What Carroty Does Differently#

Carroty is a nutrition-focused meal planning app. That’s not a tagline—it’s the core design principle.

Nutrition at Every Level#

  • Products: Every supermarket product includes the Big 7 nutrition facts
  • Recipes: Automatic nutrition calculation based on ingredients
  • Meal Plans: Real-time macro totals as you plan your week

You’re never guessing. You always know exactly what you’re eating.

Real UK Supermarket Products#

When you search for ingredients, you’re browsing actual products from Tesco and Sainsbury’s—with real prices and real nutrition data. Not generic databases. Not American equivalents.

The Complete Workflow#

  1. Discover – Find products by what you need, not just what you search for
  2. Build – Create recipes with automatic nutrition calculation
  3. Plan – Combine recipes into meal plans with live macro tracking
  4. Shop – Generate your shopping list in one click
