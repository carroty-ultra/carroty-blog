Why Carroty?

December 30, 2025 · 4 min

Why Carroty?#

There are dozens of meal planning apps available right now. We downloaded most of them. And we kept running into the same problem: they weren’t built for UK shoppers.

American grocery stores. American products. American portion sizes. Recipe databases filled with ingredients you can’t find at Tesco. Nutrition information pulled from generic databases that don’t match what’s actually on the shelves at Sainsbury’s.

For a country where millions of people do their weekly shop at UK supermarkets, the app options were surprisingly poor.

So we built Carroty.

The Problem With Most Meal Planning Apps#

They’re Built for the American Market#

The meal planning app space is dominated by US companies. That’s fine if you shop at Walmart or Kroger. But if you’re trying to plan meals around what’s available at Tesco, Sainsbury’s, or your local supermarket, you’re largely on your own.

These apps don’t know UK products. They don’t understand UK pricing. And they certainly don’t integrate with the supermarkets where you actually shop.

Product Search Is Broken#

Here’s something we noticed: traditional food search assumes you already know what you want.

Search for “chicken breast” and you’ll get chicken breast. But what if you don’t know what you want? What if you just know you need something quick? Something high in protein? Something light?

Most apps force you to think in specific products when you’re actually thinking in intentions. You’re not always searching for “Greek yoghurt”—sometimes you’re searching for “quick protein snack” and yoghurt happens to be the answer.

Nutrition Data Is a Mess#

Try finding accurate nutrition information for UK supermarket products. You’ll find generic databases, American equivalents, or nothing at all.

For anyone tracking their intake seriously—whether for health, fitness, or medical reasons—this inconsistency makes meal planning unnecessarily difficult.

What We Built Instead#

Carroty was designed from the ground up for UK shoppers. Not adapted. Not localised. Built.

Real UK Supermarket Products#

When you search for food in Carroty, you’re browsing actual products from Tesco and Sainsbury’s. Real items with real prices and real nutrition information.

This isn’t a generic food database. It’s what’s actually on the shelves at UK supermarkets.

Mood-Based Search for Products#

This is where Carroty does something genuinely different.

When you’re browsing supermarket products, you don’t have to search by product name or ingredient. You can search by what you actually need:

  • “Quick protein lunch”
  • “Post-workout snack”
  • “Low calorie breakfast”
  • “Something filling”

Carroty understands intent, not just keywords. Instead of scrolling through thousands of products trying to find something suitable, you describe what you’re looking for and Carroty finds products that match.

It’s a fundamentally different way to discover food—one that matches how people actually think when they’re staring into the fridge wondering what to eat.

Complete Nutrition Transparency#

Every product in Carroty includes the Big 7 nutrition facts per 100g/ml. We’re transparent about where our data comes from, and we include proper medical disclaimers because nutrition information matters.

The Full Workflow#

Carroty isn’t just product search. It’s the complete journey from discovery to dinner:

  1. Discover Products – Find supermarket products by mood, intent, or traditional search
  2. Build Recipes – Browse curated recipes or create your own with automatic nutrition calculation
  3. Plan Meals – Combine recipes into meal plans, adjust servings, see real-time nutrition totals
  4. Shop Smarter – Generate a complete shopping list with one click

No more copying ingredients into a notes app. No more forgetting what you needed. The workflow is connected.

Who Carroty Is For#

Carroty is for anyone in the UK who wants meal planning to be simpler:

  • Busy professionals who don’t have time to manually cross-reference recipes with supermarket websites
  • Health-conscious shoppers who want accurate nutrition data for UK products
  • Families trying to plan a week of meals without the Sunday evening stress
  • Fitness enthusiasts looking for high-protein options they can actually buy locally
  • Anyone who’s tired of American apps that don’t understand UK shopping

What’s Next#

Carroty is launching with Tesco and Sainsbury’s integration, mood-based search, recipe management, meal planning, and smart shopping lists.

But this is just the beginning. We’re building the meal planning app that UK shoppers have been waiting for, and we’re committed to making it better based on what our users actually need.
